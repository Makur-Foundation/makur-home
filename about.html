  • Welcome to Merkür!

    Merkür is a non-profit open source software foundation which gathers coders, hackers, activists, makers and designers worldwide. Also it is the Turkish name of planet Mercury, the first planet of the solar system.

    Watch Video

  • Not A Company, A Brand or A Venture, A Community!

    Merkür is not an enterpreneurship, company or a brand and is very strict about your privacy and rights. Merkür’s cause is to dominate the world with decentralised internet friendly open source products and people using them in order to crush the monopoly of companies like Microsoft, Apple and Google. The Merkür team keeps working for achieving this cause of humanity.

  • Merkür Online

    Merkür Online is a decentralized social network which is made by Merkür team over the zot protocol. At this network, every user has the permission of opening channels infinitively (depends to the status of the server). Those channels can be a profile, infographic, forum, blog, wiki and more depending to the needs of the end user. Also there’re official channels opened by Merkür team for newbies to let them socialise.

  • MerkürOS

    MerkürOS is an Arch based, modern, apt and yaourt compatible, multi-platform, libertarian operating system. It’s development is started in Turkey by Tan Siret AKINCI in order to replace pro-nationalist operating systems of the government but later it’s development is given to Merith. Now it is the main project of the Merkür team as Merith is the current project leader due to his commits. It’s gonna finish very soon as new features are coming everyday. Until that, ou can test MerkürOS at Merkür stands during open source exhibitions.
